ğŸ Yankee Swap!

Bring a wrapped gift ($20 limit) for the gift exchange!

Brave Swappers

No swappers yet. Move people to "Coming + Swapping" in the Families tab!

These are the final rules. There shall be no changes.

Gift Limit: $20
Who's Playing: One combined game including both kids and adults.
How It Works:
  1. Everyone brings a wrapped gift and puts it in the pile.
  2. Each player draws a number. That's the order in which you'll take a turn.
  3. Player 1 picks any gift from the pile and opens it.
  4. Player 2 can either:
    • steal Player 1's opened gift, or
    • choose a wrapped gift from the pile to open.
  5. Play continues in number order. On your turn, you can either steal any previously opened gift or open a new one.
  6. A gift can only be stolen once per turn, and three times total. After a gift has been stolen three times, it's "frozen" and can't be taken again.
  7. If your gift is stolen, you immediately take another turn by choosing a new wrapped gift to open.
  8. After all gifts are opened, the first player gets one last chance to steal any unfrozen gift.
Final notes: Have fun, keep it light, and aim for gifts that are kid-friendly and adult-friendly so everyone can play on equal footing.
